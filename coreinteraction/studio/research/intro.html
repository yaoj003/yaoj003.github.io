MEET THE STUDIO

Violaine et Jérémy is an illustration and graphic arts studio currently based in Paris, France.

In 2011, Jérémy Schneider and Violaine Orsoni met, fell in love, quit their jobs, and started their own studio.

WHAT DO THEY DO?

Schneider’s black-and-white illustrations are at the heart of their work. Each project begins with and revolves around a unique drawing. Drawn in felt pen, black chalk, or colored pencil, the illustrations are as meticulous as they are expressive.
